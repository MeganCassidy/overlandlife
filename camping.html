Grilled Chicken Tacos


Tacos are a tasty and fuss free meal around any campfire. Try a quick citrus marinade to add a blast of summer flavor to the grilled chicken. The marinade can be made before your trip, meaning all you have to do is throw the chicken on the grill when you’re at the campsite!


Campfire Nachos


Whether you want a main meal or a quick snack, what could be easier than loading a Dutch oven full of tortilla chips, melty cheese, salsa, and all your favorite nacho toppings and calling it dinner?!


Sweet Potato and Chili


Whip up a super easy vegetarian chili to accompany coal-roasted sweet potatoes. This meal is a prefect ending to any busy day on the road. This will keep for a few days in your cooler box so make before you leave for a quick meal or if you have more time, cook over your favourite stove.
