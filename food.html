Breakfast Omelette


Crack a few eggs, whip them up, pour into you pan and cook up the ultimate breakfast omelette with a filling of your choice. For a super fast breakfast, plain egg is the order of the day. For added flavour, try onions, bacon or cheese as a filling for a delicious way to start the day.


Breakfast Burritos


Cook up some peppers, scrambled egg and sausage. Add to a tortilla wrap for a delicious, hand-held breakfast burrito. Use ingredients already in your camping kitchen, the breakfast burrito is a quick and filling way to start the day.


Porridge


Packed with tones of nutrients, porridge is an excellent fuel food that will set you up nicely for the day whilst out exploring. With no cooking it’s super convenient, and you can add almost anything to it. Trying adding jam, honey or chocolate spread for a delicious camping breakfast!

Pasta Salad


Perk up lunch with a gorgeously creamy pasta salad. Customise with cooked veg such as peas, green beans and carrots, as well as an assortment of protein. Chicken, tuna, quorn, hard-boiled egg or cheese are all great options, depending on what you have available.


Campfire Soup


If you fancy something warm and comforting for lunch, why not stir up a tasty soup, a veg-packed curry or even some Swedish-style meatballs. Make before you go or whip up leftovers over the campfire. With an endless amount of variations this will be sure to keep you warm on those chilly winter days…


A Simple Wrap


For an alternative to sandwiches try a super tasty and colourful salad wraps. Add lots of crunchy veg for texture, whilst cheddar cheese and hummus give a flavour hit. Alternatively, add tuna, chicken or egg.

Grilled Chicken Tacos


Tacos are a tasty and fuss free meal around any campfire. Try a quick citrus marinade to add a blast of summer flavor to the grilled chicken. The marinade can be made before your trip, meaning all you have to do is throw the chicken on the grill when you’re at the campsite!


Campfire Nachos


Whether you want a main meal or a quick snack, what could be easier than loading a Dutch oven full of tortilla chips, melty cheese, salsa, and all your favorite nacho toppings and calling it dinner?!


Sweet Potato and Chili


Whip up a super easy vegetarian chili to accompany coal-roasted sweet potatoes. This meal is a prefect ending to any busy day on the road. This will keep for a few days in your cooler box so make before you leave for a quick meal or if you have more time, cook over your favourite stove.
