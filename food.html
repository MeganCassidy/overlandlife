Breakfast Omelette

Crack a few eggs, whip them up, pour into you pan and cook up the ultimate breakfast omelette with a filling of your choice. For a super fast breakfast, plain egg is the order of the day. For added flavour, try onions, bacon or cheese as a filling for a delicious way to start the day.


Breakfast Burritos

Cook up some peppers, scrambled egg and sausage. Add to a tortilla wrap for a delicious, hand-held breakfast burrito. Use ingredients already in your camping kitchen, the breakfast burrito is a quick and filling way to start the day.


Porridge

Packed with tones of nutrients, porridge is an excellent fuel food that will set you up nicely for the day whilst out exploring. With no cooking it’s super convenient, and you can add almost anything to it. Trying adding jam, honey or chocolate spread for a delicious camping breakfast!
