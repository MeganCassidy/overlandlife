There are so many beautiful campsites all across the UK, it can be difficult to chose the right one.

There are lots of things to consider from location, facilities and privacy. Or you may decide to opt for the more adventurous option and find an overnight carpark or layby to rest your head.

You can find all the information you need about campsites and free overnight stops at Pitchup or Park4Night.



Things to consider:


Wild camping... There are many campsites across the country that offer a wild camping experience. Be sure to pack everything you need as you will not likely find any facilities in these places.

Location... Some places can be very popular at certain times of the year. Make sure you plan and book ahead when camping in peak season.

Whats allowed... Some sites may have restriction on what you can bring with you. Check before you travel to aviod dissapointment.
